Common Questions

STRAIGHT ANSWERS.

Will customers know they're not talking to a person?

The system is transparent — it does not pretend to be a human dispatcher. It handles the call professionally and collects the information quickly. In practice, callers in an emergency care more about getting a response than who is responding. Being picked up beats going to voicemail every time.

I already have voicemail. Why isn't that enough?

Voicemail requires the caller to leave a message. Most people in an emergency don't — they hang up and call the next option. The First-Response Capture System keeps the caller engaged, gathers their details, and notifies you promptly. You can call back quickly, before they've already booked someone else.

I only miss calls occasionally. Is this worth it?

That depends on the value of those calls. Use the calculator above to run your own numbers. If it doesn't make financial sense for your volume, we'll tell you that directly on the call — before any next step is discussed.

How long does setup take?

Most businesses are live within 3 to 5 business days. You complete a short intake form at the start. We handle the rest. You do not need to change your phone number, install any hardware, or set aside time for a technical process. You review and approve before it goes live.

Does this replace my office staff or dispatcher?

No. This handles missed calls — the ones that currently get no response at all. If you have staff available, they answer first. The First-Response Capture System only activates when a call goes unanswered. It is a backup that runs when your team cannot get to it.

What does it cost?

Pricing is based on your call volume and setup requirements. We do not quote before understanding your situation. We will cover it on the 15-minute call — after we have looked at your numbers together.

What if I want to cancel?

Month-to-month agreements are standard. We do not lock clients into long contracts because a system that's working pays for itself. If you want to leave, you can. We will cover the full terms on the call.
